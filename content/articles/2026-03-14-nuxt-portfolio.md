Creating a single project that serves as both a portfolio and a blog is one of the best ways to stay motivated while building in public.

## Why Nuxt?

Nuxt gives you server-side rendering, automatic routing, and a smooth developer experience out of the box. With `@nuxt/ui`, you get a set of accessible UI primitives that are ready to use.

## What’s in this site

- ✅ A responsive header, theme toggle, and smooth transitions.
- ✅ Content driven by Markdown files in `content/articles`.
- ✅ Search-friendly SEO and social previews.
- ✅ Nice defaults for dark mode, animations, and responsiveness.
